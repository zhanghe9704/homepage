Welcome to He Zhang's Home Page.

Research

My research interests lie in the fields of accelerator physics and computational physics, including beam dynamics, collective effect, fast algorithm for electromagnetic field calculation, optimization, high performance parallel computation, and especially the topics related to fast multipole algorithm and differential algorithm.

Education

  • Ph.D in Physics, Physics and Astronomy Department, Michigan State University, 2006 - 2012
  • Doctor degree in Engineering, National Synchrotron Radiation Laboratory, University of Science and Technology of China, 2001 - 2006
  • B.S. in Applied Physics, Modern Physics Department, University of Science and Technology of China, 1997 - 2001
